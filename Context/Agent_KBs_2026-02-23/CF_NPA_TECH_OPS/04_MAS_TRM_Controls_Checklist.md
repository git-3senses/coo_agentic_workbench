# MAS TRM Guidelines — Controls Checklist for NPA (POC)
**Agent:** CF_NPA_TECH_OPS  
**Suggested Dify dataset:** `COO Command Center — Tech/Ops (TRM)`  
**Last updated:** 2026-02-23

## Purpose
Translate MAS Technology Risk Management (TRM) guideline themes into an NPA-ready checklist.

---

## 1) Core control areas to document in the NPA
- identity and access management (RBAC, MFA, PAM for privileged access)
- secure configuration and patching
- logging, monitoring, and incident response
- encryption and key management
- change management and SDLC controls
- resilience: backups, DR, RTO/RPO, testing
- third-party security controls and oversight

---

## 2) Agent questions (minimum)
- What systems are changed/introduced?
- What data classes are processed (incl. personal data)?
- What authentication/authorization model is used?
- What logs are produced and where stored?
- What is the DR plan and last test evidence?
- What vendor controls are in place?
