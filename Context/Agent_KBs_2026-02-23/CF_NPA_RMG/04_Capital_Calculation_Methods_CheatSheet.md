## Purpose
Provide a compact reference of what capital questions to ask and what outputs to produce.

---

## 1) Common capital “buckets” that can change with a new product
- credit risk RWA (counterparty + settlement + issuer)
- market risk capital (trading book sensitivities / VaR / stressed measures as applicable)
- CVA risk (counterparty valuation adjustment)
- operational risk (new processes, new platforms, manual workarounds)

---

## 2) Netting / collateral / CSA questions
- Is the trade under a netting agreement? Which standard (ISDA/GMRA/etc.)?
- What collateral types are eligible? What haircuts?
- How is margin calculated and called? Frequency?
