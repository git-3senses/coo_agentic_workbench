## Part C: Product Information to be Completed by Proposing Unit

### I. Product Specifications (Basic Information)

#### 1. Description:

**a) Purpose or Rationale for Proposal:**
The Digital Currency Trading Platform addresses the growing institutional demand for secure, regulated digital asset trading capabilities. This platform will enable DBS to:
- Capture significant revenue opportunities in the digital assets market (estimated $2.3B annually)
- Provide institutional clients with professional-grade digital currency trading services
- Position DBS as a leader in digital finance innovation
- Meet increasing client demand for cryptocurrency exposure and trading

**b) Scope and Parameters of Product/Service:**

**Role of PU:** Principal trader and platform operator

**Product Features:**
- Currency Denomination: BTC, ETH, ADA, SOL, and major stablecoins (USDT, USDC)
- Funding Type: Funded trading with real-time settlement
- Product Maturity/Tenor: Spot trading with T+0 settlement
- Repricing Information: Real-time market pricing with dynamic spreads
- Trading Hours: 24/7 operation with planned maintenance windows

**Product Life Cycle:**
- Platform setup and regulatory approval (6 months)
- Pilot launch with select clients (3 months)
- Full commercial launch
- Continuous enhancement and expansion

**c) Expected Transaction Volume and Revenue per Annum:**
- Year 1: $500M transaction volume, $15M revenue
- Year 2: $1.2B transaction volume, $35M revenue
- Year 3: $2.8B transaction volume, $75M revenue

**d) Business Model:**
- Revenue Drivers: Trading spreads (0.25-0.75%), custody fees (0.15% annually), platform access fees
- Cost Components: Technology infrastructure, regulatory compliance, custody operations, market making
- Target ROI: 35% by Year 3

**e) Special Purpose Vehicles (SPV):**
DBS Digital Assets Trading Pte Ltd (Singapore incorporation) for regulatory segregation and risk isolation

#### 2. Target Customer:

**Target Customer Segments:**
- Institutional Investors (Hedge funds, Family offices, Pension funds)
- Corporate Treasury departments seeking digital asset exposure
- High Net Worth Individuals (>$10M AUM)
- Professional trading firms and market makers

**Regulatory Restrictions:**
- Accredited Investors only (Singapore Securities & Futures Act)
- Institutional Professional Investor qualification required
- Enhanced KYC and AML requirements for digital assets

**Target Customer Profile:**
- Annual turnover: >$100M
- Sophisticated investors with digital asset experience
- Strong compliance and risk management frameworks
- Geographic focus: Singapore, Hong Kong, selected international markets

#### 3. Commercialization Approach:

**a) Channel Availability:**
- Dedicated digital asset trading portal (web and API)
- Relationship Manager support for onboarding and education
- 24/7 trading desk support for institutional clients

**b) Sales Suitability:**
- Comprehensive digital asset risk assessment
- Mandatory client education program
- Sophisticated investor qualification process
- Enhanced suitability documentation

**c) Marketing & Communication:**
- Institutional client seminars and education sessions
- Digital asset market research and insights
- Thought leadership content and regulatory updates
- Targeted outreach to qualified institutional investors

### II. Operational & Technology Information

#### 1. Operational Information

**a) Operating Model:**
The platform operates as an integrated digital asset trading and custody solution with the following key components:

**Front Office:** Client onboarding, trading interface, relationship management
**Middle Office:** Risk monitoring, compliance oversight, trade surveillance
**Back Office:** Settlement, custody operations, reporting
**Technology:** Platform infrastructure, security operations, data management

**Key Operational Flows:**
1. Client Onboarding → Enhanced KYC/AML → Platform Access Provisioning
2. Order Placement → Risk Checks → Market Execution → Settlement
3. Custody Operations → Security Monitoring → Regulatory Reporting

**b) Booking Process in Operations:**

**Legal Form:** ISDA Master Agreement with Digital Assets Annex
**Family:** CRY (Cryptocurrency)
**Group:** DGTL (Digital Assets)
**Type:** SPOT (Spot Trading)
**Typology (Contract):** CRY_SPOT_TRADE
**Portfolio:** DBSSG_GFM_CRYPTO
**Counterparty:** Institutional clients with appropriate digital asset agreements

**Transaction Flow:**
1. Client order submission via trading platform
2. Real-time risk and compliance checks
3. Market execution through liquidity providers
4. Automatic settlement via custody infrastructure
5. Trade confirmation and reporting
6. Regulatory transaction reporting
