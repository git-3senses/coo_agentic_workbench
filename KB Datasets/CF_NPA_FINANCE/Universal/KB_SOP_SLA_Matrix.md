## Escalation thresholds

| Condition | Trigger | Action |
|---|---|---|
| Stage breach | Stage exceeds SLA by 25% | Notify owner + maker |
| Material breach | Stage exceeds SLA by 50% | Escalate to checker lead |
| Critical breach | Any stage exceeds SLA by 100% | Escalate to COO forum |

---

## Evidence requirements for SLA exceptions
When granting an SLA extension, record:
- rationale (why delay is acceptable)
- risk assessment (what could break)
- compensating controls (what mitigates risk)
- revised due date
- approver identity
